FAQ

Your Questions Answered.

Q: What is Moodi?

MOODI is a clothing rental subscription service that allows you to check out trendy, upscale clothes every month.
We've all experienced the moment when we realize there's nothing exciting left in the closet or that we've been wearing the same item over and over again. Moodi helps to ease that pain by allowing you to check out items monthly, and keep your closet looking fresh.

Q: How much does it cost?

We currently offer two subscription plans. 3 item boxes for $50/month, and for 5 item boxes for $75/month. Boxes are delivered once a month (free of cost) by our Moodi Drivers.

Q: Are clothes cleaned prior to and do I need to wash before sending it back?

We will wash and do a quality check on all items. Our goal is to deliver high class, quality apparel for our subscribers.

Q: Can I purchase the item if I like it?

Yes! Each item has a price and is available for purchase. Simply select to keep the item at the end of the month and we will automatically bill you.

Q: When do I need to select the clothes for the following month?

We ship the clothes on the 1st, and require you to select your items for the following month by the 15th. This will allow us sufficient time to prepare for on time delivery.

Q: What if I damage an item?

In order to maintain a high standard of clothing and deliver exceptional service, we will do a quality check on all items sent and returned. If an apparel item is damaged, aside from regular wear and tear, we will send you a quality check report and the damage fee, along with time to discuss with one of our Moodi reps.

Q: Can I keep the shirt for longer than a month?

Yes, you may select to keep any clothing as long as you'd like - it will just count as an item for that month.

Q: What if I want to cancel?

We understand there will be months where you don't need to dress Moodi - so we've made cancellations very simple. You can go to your account setting and unsubscribe with just a few clicks, although we hope you return soon!
